National Innovation Diploma Programme

The post-UTME application fee for the NID Programme is ₦2,000. This amount is a non-refundable fee. Click here to start your application process.

After a successful registration process, a candidate is given provisional admission and is consequently required to pay an acceptance fee of ₦20,000 to validate his or her admission.




Professional Certificate Programme

Every student who wishes to pursue a professional certificate programme at SQI College of ICT is required to pay a non-refundable application fee of ₦6,000 Click here to start your application

Professional Certificate programs at SQI College of ICT have been structured into levels following our new curriculum and the fee for each level can be found on the course page. Click here to view our course list

Since each course is structured into levels, the cumulative fee for each course is based on the number of levels each course contains.

Executive Professional Certificate Programme

Every student who wishes to pursue a professional certificate programme at SQI College of ICT is required to pay a non-refundable application fee of ₦6,000 Click here to start your application

Professional Certificate programs at SQI College of ICT have been structured into levels following our new curriculum and the fee for each level can be found on the course page. Click here to view our course list

Since each course is structured into levels, the cumulative fee for each course is based on the number of levels each course contains.
Please note that the college operates a non-refund policy. Therefore, all monies paid to the college with the intention of partaking in any of her programs are non-refundable.
